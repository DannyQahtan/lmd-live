Paint Correction

Paint Correction

Over time, vehicle paint develops swirl marks, fine scratches, oxidation, and other imperfections that reduce its shine and overall appearance. Our paint correction process carefully removes these defects to restore depth, clarity, and gloss, bringing your vehicle's finish back to its best condition. The result is a smoother, richer, mirror-like finish that enhances the vehicle's appearance and prepares the surface for long-term protection.

  • Paint depth measurement
  • Single or multi-stage machine polish
  • Swirl mark removal
  • Fine scratch correction
  • Oxidation & water spot removal
  • Pre-wax surface preparation

Ceramic Coating

Interior & exterior surfaces.

Ceramic Coating

Our premium ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection for both your vehicle's interior and exterior surfaces. Applied to paintwork, glass, wheels, trim, leather, plastics, and fabrics, it creates a durable protective layer that repels water, dirt, UV rays, and everyday contaminants. The coating enhances gloss, simplifies maintenance, and helps preserve your vehicle's appearance, keeping it cleaner, protected, and looking its best for years to come.

Applicable surfaces

  • Paint
  • Glass
  • Plastic trim
  • Wheels
  • Headlights
  • Matte surfaces
  • Leather
  • Alcantara
  • Fabric

Interior Polishing

Interior Polishing

Dashboard plastics, door cards, centre console, and trim panels fade and scratch over time. We remove fine scratches and scuffs from hard plastic and gloss trim, restoring colour and clarity throughout the cabin.

  • Dashboard & console polishing
  • Door card trim restoration
  • Gloss trim scratch removal
  • Plastic surface correction
  • UV-protective dressing applied

Plastic Trim Restoration

Plastic Trim Restoration

Exterior plastic trim fades and turns grey with UV exposure. We apply a professional-grade treatment that chemically bonds to the surface, restoring colour and providing UV protection to prevent future fading.

  • Exterior trim degreasing
  • Surface preparation
  • Professional-grade trim restorer
  • UV protection bonded to surface
  • Wheel arches, door trims, bumpers

Headlight Restoration

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy, yellowed headlights reduce road visibility and look poor. We wet-sand through multiple grades, machine polish, and apply a UV sealant to restore clarity — at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

  • Wet sanding (multiple grades)
  • Machine polish
  • UV protective sealant applied
  • Before & after documentation
  • Improves light output & safety
